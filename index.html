Iran Darroudi

Iran Darroudi (2 September 1936 – 29 October 2021) was an Iranian contemporary artist. Her art consists of surreal paintings dealing with Iranian themed imagery and strong lighting. She lived between Tehran and Paris for many years.This project is dedicated to her and her art. To know more about her please click here.
